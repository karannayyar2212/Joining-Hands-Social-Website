About Us

Welcome To Joining Hands

Joining Hands is a platform which aims to solve the problem of hunger, malnutrition and food wastage in the country. We channelize extra food from individuals, weddings, restaurants, corporate offices, events to the NGOs and other such organisations who can give it to people who really need it and have no means or access to food. There are many organizations in the country who are working towards this common goal but they are all scattered and hence as a result are unaccesed by many. We aim to solve this by providing a common platform to all of them.
