What we can accept

  • Excess product
  • Excess food from events and marriage
  • Food from school and hostel
  • Hygienic food from homes
  • whole and raw grains
  • Unsold food from restaurants and bakeries

What we don't accept

  • Regulated products such as alcohol, tobacco or drugs of dependence
  • Product past its use by date
  • Product more than 6 months past its best before date
  • Mouldy or rotting product
  • Unhygienic food
